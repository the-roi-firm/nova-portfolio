About Shan

"Not a brand.
A person.
That's the difference."

Nova exists because of a simple belief - that good grooming should never feel like a gamble.

Shan has spent years developing something that most salons don't offer: the ability to read a person. To understand not just the cut they describe, but the result they actually want. The gap between those two things is where most haircuts go wrong.

At Nova, every client works directly with Shan - from the first message to the final look. No assistants, no handoffs, no "your usual stylist isn't available today." The limited availability isn't a restriction. It's a guarantee that when you sit down, you have Shan's complete attention.
